\begin{abstract}
    This paper describes the central findings of using UPPAAL to model the Gear Controller described in \citetitle{gearcontrol} by \citeauthor{gearcontrol}.\\
    The paper will highlight the usability of UPPAAL, the relevance of the case study to the group's understanding of model checking, identified properties which the use case does not cover, failing properties, and lastly further explorations made while working with UPPAAL.\\
\end{abstract}
